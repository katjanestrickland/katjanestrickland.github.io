About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher working with Dr. Wei Li at the University of Florida. I completed my Ph.D. in Quantitative Methods at the University of Pennsylvania Graduate School of Education, where I was advised by Dr. Wendy Chan. My research focuses on developing and applying quantitative methods to address questions in education policy, with a particular emphasis on causal inference and longitudinal study design in urban education settings.

Research Interests

  • Causal inference
  • Education policy
  • Urban education
  • Longitudinal study design
