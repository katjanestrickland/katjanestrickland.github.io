About

I am a postdoctoral researcher advised by Dr. Wei Li at the University of Florida. I completed my Ph.D. in Quantitative Methods at the University of Pennsylvania and M.A. in Statistics at the Wharton School, where I worked with Drs. Wendy Chan and Michael Gottfried. My research focuses on developing and applying quantitative methods to address questions in education policy, with a particular emphasis on causal inference and longitudinal study design in urban education settings.

Research Interests

  • Causal inference
  • Education policy
  • Urban education
  • Longitudinal study design
